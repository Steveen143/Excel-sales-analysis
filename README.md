# Excel-sales-analysis


This project presents a sales performance analysis for Kultra Mega Stores using Microsoft Excel. The objective was to analyze sales trends, customer behavior, and operational efficiency to generate actionable business insights.

🛠️ TOOLS USED
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Pivot Charts


📊 KEY ANALYSIS PERFORMED
Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Cost
Sales by Channels
Sales by Warehouse
Sales by Products
Top 10 Customners
Sales by period


🔍 KEY INSIGHTS

Chase Express channel displayed the highest sales
Higher sales are mostly recorded towrds the fourth quater of each yer
High shipping costs were not always aligned with order priority
A small group of customers contributed most of the profit


💡 RECOMMENDATIONS
Focus marketing efforts on high-performing categories
Improve sales strategies in low-performing regions
Optimize shipping decisions based on order priority
Strengthen relationships with high-value customers
